About URL Slugs

A URL slug is the part of a URL that identifies a particular page in a human-readable format. Good slugs are essential for SEO and user experience.

Best Practices

  • Keep it short: 3-5 words is ideal
  • Use hyphens: Separate words with hyphens, not underscores
  • Lowercase only: Avoid uppercase letters
  • Remove stop words: Remove "a", "the", "and", etc.
  • Include keywords: Use relevant keywords for SEO
  • Avoid special characters: Stick to letters, numbers, and hyphens

Common Use Cases

  • Blog post URLs
  • Article permalinks
  • Product page URLs
  • Category and tag pages
  • Landing pages
